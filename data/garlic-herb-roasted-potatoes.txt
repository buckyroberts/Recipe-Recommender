Garlic Herb Roasted Potatoes

Ingredients:
- 2 lbs baby potatoes, halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 4 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tsp dried rosemary
- 2 tsp dried thyme
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
2. In a large mixing bowl, combine olive oil, minced garlic, dried rosemary, dried thyme, salt, and pepper.
3. Toss the halved baby potatoes in the mixture, ensuring each potato is well coated.
4. Spread potatoes on a baking sheet in a single layer.
5. Roast for 25-30 minutes or until potatoes are golden brown and tender, stirring halfway through.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!
