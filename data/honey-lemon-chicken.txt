Honey Lemon Chicken

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts
- 2 tablespoons honey
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix honey and lemon juice.
2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides.
5. Pour the honey lemon mixture over the chicken and continue cooking for another 5-7 minutes.
6. Serve hot.
