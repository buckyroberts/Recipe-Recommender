Garlic Parmesan Asparagus

Ingredients:
- 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. In a mixing bowl, toss asparagus with olive oil, minced garlic, salt, and pepper.
3. Spread the asparagus in a single layer on a baking sheet.
4. Roast in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until tender.
5. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese and return to the oven for 2-3 minutes or until the cheese is melted and slightly golden.
6. Serve hot.
