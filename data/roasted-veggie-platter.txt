Roasted Veggie Platter

Ingredients:
- 2 cups assorted vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, carrots, broccoli)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
2. Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme.
3. Spread the vegetables on a baking tray in a single layer.
4. Roast for 25-30 minutes or until the vegetables are tender and slightly caramelized.
5. Serve hot.
