Honey Glazed Pork

Ingredients:
- 2 pork chops
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, mix together honey, soy sauce, garlic powder, and onion powder to make the marinade.
2. Season the pork chops with salt and pepper, then pour the marinade over them. Let them marinate for at least 30 minutes.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the pork chops and cook until browned on both sides.
4. Pour the remaining marinade over the pork and continue cooking until the pork is cooked through and the sauce has thickened.
5. Serve the pork chops with the sauce poured over them.
