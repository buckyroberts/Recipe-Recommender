Lemon Butter Pasta

Ingredients:
- 200g pasta of choice
- 2 tablespoons butter
- Juice of 1 lemon
- Zest of 1 lemon
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley, chopped for garnish

Instructions:
1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
2. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add the lemon juice and zest to the skillet and stir to combine.
4. Toss in the cooked pasta and stir until well coated with the lemon butter sauce.
5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
