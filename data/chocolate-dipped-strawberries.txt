Chocolate Dipped Strawberries

Ingredients:
- 200g dark chocolate
- 15 fresh strawberries, washed and dried
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Instructions:
1. Melt the dark chocolate with coconut oil in a microwave or over a double boiler.
2. Dip each strawberry into the melted chocolate, ensuring it's coated well.
3. Place the dipped strawberries on a tray lined with parchment paper.
4. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes or until the chocolate is set.
5. Serve and enjoy.
