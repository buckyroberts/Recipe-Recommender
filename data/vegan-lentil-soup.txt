Vegan Lentil Soup

Ingredients:
- 1 cup dried lentils, washed and drained
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 cups vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Add onions, carrots, and celery. Sauté until vegetables are tender.
3. Add minced garlic and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.
4. Stir in the lentils, vegetable broth, and ground cumin.
5. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 25-30 minutes or until lentils are soft.
6. Season with salt and pepper. Serve hot.
