Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:
- 4 bell peppers, tops removed and seeds cleaned out
- 200g ground beef or turkey
- 1 cup cooked rice
- 1/2 cup tomato sauce
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Grated cheese for topping (optional)

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
3. Add ground beef or turkey and cook until browned.
4. Stir in the cooked rice, tomato sauce, oregano, salt, and pepper.
5. Stuff each bell pepper with the meat and rice mixture.
6. Place the stuffed peppers in a baking dish and cover with aluminum foil.
7. Bake for 25-30 minutes. If desired, sprinkle cheese on top in the last 10 minutes of baking.
8. Serve hot.
