Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Ingredients:
- 1 cup rolled oats
- 2 cups water or milk
- 1 apple, diced
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 2 tbsp maple syrup or honey
- Nuts or seeds for topping (optional)

Instructions:
1. In a saucepan, bring water or milk to a boil.
2. Add rolled oats and reduce the heat to medium.
3. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the oats are soft and have absorbed most of the liquid.
4. Stir in diced apple, cinnamon, and maple syrup or honey.
5. Serve hot, topped with nuts or seeds if desired.
