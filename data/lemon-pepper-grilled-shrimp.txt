Lemon Pepper Grilled Shrimp

Ingredients:
- 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Zest and juice of 1 lemon
- 1 tsp black pepper
- Salt, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, combine olive oil, lemon zest, lemon juice, black pepper, and salt.
2. Add the shrimp and toss to coat.
3. Marinate for 15-20 minutes.
4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
5. Grill the shrimp for 2-3 minutes on each side or until they are opaque and cooked through.
6. Serve with lemon wedges.
