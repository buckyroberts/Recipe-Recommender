Roasted Red Pepper Hummus

Ingredients:
- 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
- 2 roasted red peppers
- 2 tbsp tahini
- 1 lemon, juiced
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, roasted red peppers, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic cloves.
2. Process until smooth, adding olive oil gradually.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Serve with pita chips or fresh vegetables.
