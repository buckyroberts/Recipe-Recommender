Sesame Noodle Bowl

Ingredients:
- 200g noodles of choice
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon chili flakes
- Green onions, chopped for garnish
- Sesame seeds for garnish

Instructions:
1. Cook the noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
2. In a bowl, whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, honey, peanut butter, and chili flakes to make the sauce.
3. Toss the cooked noodles in the sauce until well coated.
4. Serve the noodles garnished with chopped green onions and sesame seeds.
