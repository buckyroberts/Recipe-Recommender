Garlic Herb Salmon

Ingredients:
- 2 salmon fillets
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:
1. Season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add the salmon fillets to the skillet, skin-side down. Cook until the skin is crispy.
4. Flip the salmon and cook until the other side is golden and the salmon is cooked through.
5. Serve the salmon with lemon wedges on the side.
