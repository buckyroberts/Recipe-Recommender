Balsamic Bruschetta

Ingredients:
- 1 baguette, sliced
- 2 cups cherry tomatoes, quartered
- 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
- Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:
1. In a bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
2. Let the mixture sit for about 10 minutes to marinate.
3. Toast the baguette slices until they are golden brown.
4. Top each slice with the tomato mixture.
5. Serve immediately.
