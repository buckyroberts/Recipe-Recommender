Teriyaki Beef Stir-Fry

Ingredients:
- 200g beef strips
- 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon sesame seeds

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
3. Add beef strips and cook until browned.
4. Pour in teriyaki sauce and stir well.
5. Add bell pepper and onion slices. Cook until vegetables are tender.
6. Sprinkle sesame seeds over the top before serving.
