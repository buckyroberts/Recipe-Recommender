Basil Pesto Chicken

Ingredients:
- 2 chicken breasts
- 2 tablespoons basil pesto
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish

Instructions:
1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until browned on both sides.
3. Spread the basil pesto over the chicken breasts. Add the cherry tomatoes to the skillet.
4. Cover the skillet and let the chicken cook through.
5. Serve the chicken with the cherry tomatoes and garnish with grated Parmesan cheese.
