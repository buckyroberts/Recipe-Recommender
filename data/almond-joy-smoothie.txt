Almond Joy Smoothie

Ingredients:
- 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
- 1 banana
- 2 tbsp almond butter
- 1 tbsp cocoa powder
- 1 tbsp shredded coconut
- 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
- Ice cubes

Instructions:
1. In a blender, combine coconut milk, banana, almond butter, cocoa powder, shredded coconut, honey or maple syrup (if using), and ice cubes.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
