Creamy Coconut Curry

Ingredients:
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons red curry paste
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 200g tofu, cubed
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions:
1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add onions and bell peppers. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.
3. Stir in the red curry paste and cook for 2-3 minutes.
4. Add tofu cubes and stir well to coat with the curry paste.
5. Pour in the coconut milk and soy sauce. Bring to a simmer.
6. Let it cook for 10-12 minutes, stirring occasionally.
7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh cilantro.
