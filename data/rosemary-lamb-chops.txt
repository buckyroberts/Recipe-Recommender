Rosemary Lamb Chops

Ingredients:
- 4 lamb chops
- 2 teaspoons dried rosemary
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Season the lamb chops with salt, pepper, and dried rosemary.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
3. Place the lamb chops in the skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side until browned.
4. Serve hot.
