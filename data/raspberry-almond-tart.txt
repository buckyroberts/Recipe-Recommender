Raspberry Almond Tart

Ingredients:
- 1 pre-made pie crust
- 1 cup raspberries
- 1/4 cup almond paste
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- Sliced almonds for garnish

Instructions:
1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
2. Roll out the pie crust and press it into a tart pan.
3. In a bowl, combine almond paste, sugar, egg, and vanilla extract. Spread this mixture over the pie crust.
4. Top with raspberries and sliced almonds.
5. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden and the filling is set.
6. Allow to cool before serving.
